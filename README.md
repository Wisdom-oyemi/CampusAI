**CampusAI**
An AI agent, built using NVIDIA Nemotron, that helps students find timely resources such as tutoring, campus events, and deadlines for clubs and assignments. 

**Overview**
Campus life generates an overwhelming amount of informationâ€”emails, learning management systems, group chats, Instagram posts, and physical flyers. Important opportunities and deadlines are often missed simply because they are scattered across too many platforms.

CampusAI was conceptualized during a hackathon by Clue, Oyemi, Stephenson, and Thomas to address this problem. The goal is to provide students with a single, intelligent point of access to campus resources that are supposedly created "just for them", but are often difficult to find when needed.

Using NVIDIA Nemotron, CampusAI can answer campus-related questions and integrate with external APIs to surface relevant events, tutoring options, and deadlines at the right time.

**Features**
- Natural-language Q&A for campus-related questions
- Centralized access to tutoring resources and academic support
- Aggregated campus events and club deadlines
- API-based event syncing (planned)
- Designed for extensibility across different campuses
